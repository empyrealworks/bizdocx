Data Deletion

BizDocx  ·  Empyreal Works  ·  Last updated June 2025

🗂️  Signed-in users can delete their account and all associated cloud data directly from within the app. Requests are processed immediately — deletion is instant and irreversible.

📱  Local cache (PDFs and images stored on your device) is not affected by account deletion. To remove local data, clear app data from your device settings or uninstall the app.

How to Delete Your Account & Data

Follow these steps inside the BizDocx app. You do not need to contact us separately — the in-app flow is the official deletion channel.

1
Open Settings

Tap the settings icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner of the home screen.

2
Scroll to "Account"

Make sure you are signed in. Your account section is near the bottom of the settings list.

3
Tap "Delete My Data"

A confirmation dialog will appear explaining exactly what will be deleted.

4
Confirm deletion

Tap Delete Everything. You will be signed out immediately and all cloud data will be removed.

Can't access the app? Email us at hello@empyrealworks.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and your account email address. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once confirmed, your portfolios, documents, and account cannot be recovered.

Data Location Deleted?
Firebase account & sign-in credentials Firebase Authentication Yes
User profile (tier, credits, usage counters) Cloud Firestore Yes
All business portfolios Cloud Firestore Yes
All generated documents & version history Cloud Firestore Yes
Business portfolio AI context Cloud Firestore Yes
Uploaded logos & generated image assets Firebase Storage Yes
Locally cached PDFs & images on device Device storage only Not affected
App preferences & theme settings Device storage only Not affected

Locally cached documents stored on your device are not deleted automatically. To remove them, clear the app's storage from your device settings or uninstall BizDocx.

Deleting Individual Documents

You can delete individual documents without deleting your entire account. Long-press any document tile on the Portfolio screen to reveal the delete button, then confirm deletion. This removes the document from Firestore and, for logo and icon assets, from Firebase Storage.

Timeline

When you delete your account via the in-app flow, deletion happens immediately — Firestore documents, Storage files, and your Firebase Auth account are all removed in a single operation. You are signed out instantly.

For deletion requests submitted via email, we process them within 30 days, in line with Google Play's data deletion policy and GDPR Article 17 requirements.

If you have a pending email request and change your mind, contact us at hello@empyrealworks.com before your request is processed.
