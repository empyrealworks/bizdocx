Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025  ·  Developed by Adeleke Olasope under Empyreal Works

🔒  Your business data stays private. We store only what's needed to run the app and never sell or share your information with advertisers.

🤖  AI generation sends your business context and prompt to Google's Gemini API — only when you tap Generate. Audio or sensitive file data is never transmitted.

☁️  All documents and portfolios are stored securely in Firebase with offline persistence — accessible even without an internet connection.

🚫  No ads. No analytics SDKs. No third-party tracking.

1. Who We Are

BizDocx is developed and maintained by Adeleke Olasope, trading as Empyreal Works. For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@empyrealworks.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

Managed entirely by Firebase Authentication. Supported sign-in methods are email/password and Google Sign-In. We do not store passwords directly.

Business portfolio data

When you create a portfolio, the following information is stored in your private Firestore document:

  • Business name, description, mission statement, and target audience
  • Brand colour codes
  • Company logo (stored in Firebase Storage as an image file)
  • Document IDs referencing your generated assets

Generated documents

All documents you generate are stored in your private Firestore collection. This includes:

  • The document title, type, and original prompt
  • HTML content for structural documents (invoices, proposals, contracts, etc.)
  • Firebase Storage URL for graphical assets (logos, icons)
  • Version history snapshots for structural documents

Usage counters

For free-tier rate limiting, we store the date of your last document generation and image generation. This data is used only to enforce free-tier limits and is never sold or shared.

3. How We Use Your Data

AI document generation Gemini API

When you tap Generate, your business context (name, mission, brand colours, logo URL, target audience) and your document prompt are sent to Google's Gemini API or Imagen API over HTTPS.

This is the only time your data is transmitted to a third party for processing. We do not retain prompts or context data on third-party servers after the API call completes. Google's own data handling is governed by the Google Gemini API Terms of Service.

PDF rendering PDF Engine

Structural documents are rendered to PDF by a remote Playwright microservice operated by Empyreal Works. The generated HTML is sent to this service over HTTPS for conversion. No HTML content is stored by the PDF engine beyond the duration of a single request.

Everything else

Your data is used solely to provide the BizDocx service — personalising generated documents, syncing portfolios across devices, and enforcing tier limits. We do not use your data to train AI models, display ads, or share with any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Device Permissions

Permission Purpose
Internet Required for sign-in, AI generation, PDF export, and cloud sync.
Storage / Files Required to cache generated PDFs and images locally on your device for offline access.
Camera / Photo Library Required only when you choose to upload your company logo from your device gallery.

5. Third-Party Services

Service Purpose
Firebase Authentication Manages user sign-in credentials. Privacy policy
Cloud Firestore Stores your portfolios, documents, version history, and user profile in a private collection.
Firebase Storage Stores uploaded logos and AI-generated image assets (logos, icons).
Firebase App Check Verifies that requests come from a legitimate build of the app to prevent abuse.
Google Gemini API Generates structured document HTML from your prompt. Terms of service
Google Imagen API Generates logo and icon images from your prompt.
PDF Engine (Empyreal Works) Converts generated HTML documents to print-ready PDF files.

No analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or other third-party tracking services are used.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Generated documents and portfolios remain in your account until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Locally cached PDFs and images are stored on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.
  • Deleting a document removes it from Firestore and, for graphical assets, from Firebase Storage.
  • Deleting your account removes all associated portfolios, documents, version history, and your user profile. This action is permanent and irreversible.
  • To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete My Data in the app.

7. Security

  • All communication with Firebase and Google APIs uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • Firebase Security Rules restrict all Firestore and Storage reads/writes to the authenticated owner of the data.
  • Firebase App Check is enforced to prevent unauthorised API access.

No security measure is perfect. We encourage you to use a strong sign-in method and to delete sensitive documents you no longer need.

8. Children's Privacy

BizDocx is intended for use by business owners and entrepreneurs. It is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at hello@empyrealworks.com and we will delete the account promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy
